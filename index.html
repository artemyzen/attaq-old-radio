Devlog
v 0.1.11 – Added local user presets with save, load and delete actions.
v 0.1.10 – Added Created Track player with seek bar, time display, download and delete buttons. Created Track playback is connected to the oscilloscope and output meters.
v 0.1.9 – Removed distortion from the Broken Antenna preset.
v 0.1.8 – Improved off-station tuning behavior: near the edges the clean signal can fully disappear while noise and interference remain and intensify.
v 0.1.7 – Added Tuning Drift Speed and renamed Tuning Drift to Tuning Drift Range.
v 0.1.6 – Updated Broken Antenna preset with stronger tuning drift, buzz, haunt, speaker age and dropout settings.
v 0.1.5 – Radio Tuning meter now shows the actual tuning position, including automatic Tuning Drift. Removed frequency numbers from the tuning display.
v 0.1.4 – Reordered radio controls by signal flow. Tuning Drift now works as automated tuning range, moving the radio back and forth around the manual Tuning position.
v 0.1.3 – Clean Old Radio is now the default startup preset. Radio Tuning meter now reacts only to the Tuning slider. Removed the tuning graphic from the header. Added beta note and improved devlog block.
v 0.1.2 – Added manual Tuning control for realistic station searching between clean signal, static and interference.
v 0.1.1 – Reduced presets to Clean Old Radio, Broken Antenna and Reset. Presets moved into a separate block.
v 0.1.0 – First public beta of ATTAQ Old Radio.
